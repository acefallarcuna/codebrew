What is Insurance?



Insurance is a way to protect against financial loss by transferring risk to an insurer in exchange for a premium.

Insurance comes in various types, each designed to cover specific risks that individuals and organizations may face.

Health Insurance: Provides coverage for medical expenses, including doctor's visits, hospitalization, and prescription medication.

Life Insurance: Provides financial support to the beneficiaries of the insured in the event of their death.

Auto Insurance: Covers damages and injuries resulting from car accidents, and can also provide coverage for theft and vandalism.

Travel Insurance: Provides coverage for unexpected events that can occur during travel, such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost or stolen luggage.

These are just a few examples of the many types of insurance available, each designed to provide financial protection and peace of mind in different areas of life.
