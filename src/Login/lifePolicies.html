Life Insurance



ProTech StarterLife

This is a term life insurance policy that provides coverage for a specific term, usually between 10 to 30 years. It is an affordable option for young adults who may be starting their careers or families and have a limited budget. The policy provides a death benefit to the beneficiaries if the insured passes away during the term of the policy.

ProTech SecureLife

This is a whole life insurance policy that provides lifelong coverage as long as the premiums are paid. The policy also has a cash value component that grows over time and can be borrowed against or used to pay premiums. This policy provides financial security and peace of mind for Gen Z members.

ProTech FamilyLife

This is a term life insurance policy that provides coverage for a specific term, usually between 10 to 30 years. It is designed to provide financial protection for families in case the primary breadwinner passes away. The policy provides a death benefit to the beneficiaries if the insured passes away during the term of the policy.

ProTech AdventureLife

This is an accidental death and dismemberment insurance policy that provides coverage in the event of an accidental death or dismemberment. It is designed for Gen Z members who enjoy adventure sports or activities. The policy provides a benefit to the beneficiaries if the insured passes away due to an accident or if the insured loses a limb, vision, or hearing due to an accident.

ProTech LegacyLife

This is a whole life insurance policy that provides lifelong coverage as long as the premiums are paid. The policy has a cash value component that grows over time and can be used to build a legacy for future generations. The policyholder can name beneficiaries to receive the death benefit and the cash value component can be used to fund a trust or estate plan.
