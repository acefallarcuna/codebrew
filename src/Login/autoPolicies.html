ProTech BasicAuto

This is a basic auto insurance policy that provides liability coverage for bodily injury and property damage caused by the insured driver. It is an affordable option for young adults who have a limited budget and are driving an older or less expensive vehicle.

ProTech EcoAuto

This is an auto insurance policy that provides discounts for eco-friendly vehicles and safe driving habits that reduce carbon emissions. It rewards policyholders for driving less, carpooling, and using public transportation. It is an ideal option for Gen Z members who are environmentally conscious and want to reduce their carbon footprint.

ProTech CollisionProtect

This is an auto insurance policy that provides coverage for collision damage to the insured vehicle. It covers repairs or replacement of the insured vehicle if it is damaged in a collision, regardless of who is at fault. It is an ideal option for Gen Z members who have a newer or more expensive vehicle.

ProTech RoadsideAssist

This is an auto insurance policy that provides roadside assistance in case of a breakdown or other roadside emergency. It covers services such as towing, jump-starting a battery, changing a flat tire, and unlocking a vehicle. It is an ideal option for Gen Z members who may not have access to a reliable mechanic or roadside assistance service.

ProTech GeneralProtect

This is an auto insurance policy that provides coverage for non-collision damage to the insured vehicle. It covers damage from theft, fire, vandalism, and natural disasters. It is an important option for Gen Z members who live in areas prone to natural disasters or have a higher risk of vehicle theft.
