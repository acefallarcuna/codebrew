Health Insurance



ProTech BasicHealth

This is a basic health insurance policy that covers the essential health benefits required by the Affordable Care Act. It provides coverage for preventive care, hospitalization, and prescription drugs. It is an affordable option for young adults who are generally healthy and have a limited budget.

ProTech StudentHealth

This is a health insurance policy that is designed for college students. It provides coverage for preventive care, office visits, and emergency care. It is an affordable option for students who may not have access to a group health insurance plan through their school.

ProTech FamilyHealth

This is a health insurance policy that provides coverage for the entire family. It covers preventive care, office visits, hospitalization, and prescription drugs. It is designed to provide financial protection for families in case of unexpected medical expenses.

ProTech MentalHealth

This is a health insurance policy that provides coverage for mental health services. It covers counseling, therapy, and medication management for mental health conditions. It is an important option for Gen Z members who may be struggling with mental health issues.

ProTech DigitalHealth

This is a health insurance policy that provides coverage for digital health services. It covers telemedicine, remote monitoring, and other digital health services that can help improve health outcomes and reduce health care costs. It is an innovative option for Gen Z members who are tech-savvy and prefer digital health services.
