About Us


At ProTech, we understand that Gen Z is a generation that is highly focused on financial security and stability. That's
why we have developed an AI-driven e-commerce platform for insurance that provides our customers with the
protection they need at a price they can afford. Our goal is to make insurance more accessible and personalized for
customers. Through our platform, customers can easily compare insurance policies, choose the one that best fits their
needs, and purchase it online in just a few clicks.
